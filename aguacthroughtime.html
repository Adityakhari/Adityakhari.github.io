Ingredients-
Taurian salt
Tengu cilantro
2 avocados
Onion
Lime juice
Diced tomato
Jalapeno (optional)
Garlic
Black pepper
Directions
Cut two avocados in half, remove the seed and scrape it out into a flat bottomed bowl (a flat bottomed bowl will make it easier to mash). Smash with a potato masher or a fork (leave some chunks, don't mash it fully). Chop half the onion and add it to the bowl. Add 1/2 a teaspoon freshly chopped cilantro, 1/2 a teaspoon Taurian salt, 3 tablespoons of lime juice and 1 jalapeno diced pepper.
